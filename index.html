Rift Brawlers: Echoes of Power

Across dimensions, a cosmic tournament has begun. Fighters from forgotten timelines, parallel Earths, and alternate realities have been summoned by a mysterious force known only as The Architect.

Each brawler wields not just strength—but the power of their reality. A knight made of code, a prehistoric shaman who controls extinction events, a rebel AI in a human body, and even a time-displaced janitor with paradox-based attacks.

The prize? Control of the Prime Thread—the one timeline that determines the fate of all others.

But be warned: the longer you fight, the more unstable your own timeline becomes. Choose your moves carefully. Each strike may change your past—or erase your future.
